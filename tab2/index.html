Structure of the Soviet State

Understanding the administrative hierarchy, democratic centralism, and operational branches of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics. This page outlines how our mock government is organized, managed, and executed within the server.

Legislative Branch

The Supreme Soviet of the USSR

The Supreme Soviet is the highest legislative body in the Soviet Union and the sole organ authorized to amend the Constitution. Within our mock government simulation, it serves as the ultimate voting body for server-wide rules, budget allocations (Rubles), and party directives.

Soviet of the Union

Elected by citizens on the basis of population districts. Represents the general interests of all citizens regardless of nationality.

Soviet of Nationalities

Elected to represent the specific administrative divisions (Republics, Autonomous Oblasts) to ensure fair representation of all cultures.

The Presidium

When the Supreme Soviet is not in active session, the Presidium acts on its behalf, carrying out administrative decrees and managing Discord server disputes.

Executive Branch

The Council of Ministers

Acting as the highest executive and administrative organ of state power, the Council of Ministers (formerly Sovnarkom) manages the day-to-day operations of the economy, military, and internal affairs. Ministers are appointed leaders in our Discord server who manage specific channels and roleplay sectors.

Key Ministries (Active Server Departments)

  • Gosplan (State Planning Committee): Dictates production quotas, manages the bot economy, and prices state goods.
  • KGB (Committee for State Security): The server moderation team. Handles rule violations, alternative account sweeps, and server security.
  • Ministry of Agriculture & Labor: Assigns new server members their starting roles and tracks weekly activity metrics.

The Vanguard: The Communist Party

The Communist Party of the Soviet Union (CPSU) is the sole ruling party and the guiding force of Soviet society. While government organs handle administration, the Party provides ideological direction and approves all major state appointments.

The Politburo & Central Committee

The Central Committee is the highest body of the Party between Congresses. Its inner circle, the Politburo (composed of the server's head Administrators/Founders), makes the ultimate executive decisions regarding server direction, lore, and high-level roleplay events.

Union Republics & Local Soviets

The USSR is a federal state consisting of 15 supposedly voluntary Union Republics. In our server environment, these Republics act as distinct roleplay hubs or categories, each with its own local governance, chat channels, and industrial focus.
